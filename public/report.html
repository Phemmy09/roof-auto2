1 What Is Roof Auto?

Roof Auto is a private web application built exclusively for Reliable Exteriors Group. It uses Artificial Intelligence to read your roofing job documents — Eagle View reports, insurance scopes, contracts, and permits — and automatically generates a complete materials order and crew summary.

What used to take significant manual effort per job now takes seconds.

6 Formula Engine — Full Business Control

All material calculations are driven by a Formula Engine — a dedicated page where every formula can be viewed, edited, or toggled on/off without touching any code.

If your ordering standards change — for example, if you start using a different nail quantity per square — you update the formula once and it applies to every future job automatically.

💡 This gives Reliable Exteriors Group full ownership over how materials are calculated, with no dependency on a developer for routine formula adjustments.
